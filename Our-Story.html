BORN IN MEXICO,
RAISED IN L.A.

It started with a mother's recipe and one man's desire for a
quality chicken dinner after a hard day's work. Over 40
years later, we still grill our chicken the same way, citrus
marinated and grilled low and slow over an open flame. In
that time, we've fed families, connected cultures and have
become a Southern California institution.

WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

We believe that good food feeds more than just our appetites.
It nourishes the soul.

Good food brings us together. And connects us to something
larger than ourselves.

We believe that sitting down and sharing a meal prepared with
time, love and quality ingredients makes us all feel like family.

And when that happens, the world is a better place.
